1. Stratosphere - d. Coldest layer

3. Thermosphere - c. Satellites
4. Mesosphere - a. Radio waves

Section - 3: Short Answer Questions (10 Marks)

Answer in one sentence the following question (Choose any five):
1. Name an instrument used to measure the speed of the wind. An anemometer is used to measure the speed of the wind.
2. What is the atmosphere? The atmosphere is the layer of gases that surrounds the Earth.
3. What are exogenic forces? Give two examples. Exogenic forces are external forces that shape the Earth's surface. Two examples are erosion and weathering.
4. Define endogenic forces. What are tectonic plates? Endogenic forces are internal forces that shape the Earth's surface. Tectonic plates are large, rigid pieces of the Earth's lithosphere.
5. What type of rock contains fossils? Define humidity. Sedimentary rocks often contain fossils. Humidity is the amount of water vapor present in the air.
